Are you interested in contributing to freesewing? 
That's awesome!

Please stop by [our chat rooms on Discord](https://chat.freesewing.org/).
It's the best starting point to get involved with the project.

Alternatively, have a look at 
the [open issues on GitHub](https://github.com/freesewing/freesewing/issues) as
they give a good indication of the things we're working on. 

<Note>

##### Code of conduct

Freesewing strives to be an open and inclusive community.
To keep the trolls at bay, we have a [code of conduct](/guides/code-of-conduct/).  
As a contributor, you are expected to uphold it.

</Note>
